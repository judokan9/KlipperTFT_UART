A21: Home axes
title Home axes

alt Home all:
    LCD->Printer: HOME 'all'
    note over LCD,Printer: A21 C
else Home X:
    LCD->Printer: HOME 'X'
    note over LCD,Printer: A21 X
else Home Y:
    LCD->Printer: HOME 'Y'
    note over LCD,Printer: A21 Y
else Home Z:
    LCD->Printer: HOME 'Z'
    note over LCD,Printer: A21 Z
